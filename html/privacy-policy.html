Privacy Policy

Privacy policy governing data collection, processing, and protection at One Layer™

What is the Privacy Policy of One Layer?

The Privacy Policy of One Layer™ explains how personal and business data is collected, used, protected, and processed in accordance with global privacy standards and Indian data protection regulations.

This privacy policy applies to all digital properties, services, and business infrastructure solutions operated by One Layer™.

Last Updated: January 2026

One Layer respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Business details
  • Website usage data (IP address, browser, pages visited)
  • Communication data from forms, emails, or calls

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Respond to inquiries
  • Provide services
  • Improve our website and offerings
  • Send service-related communication
  • Marketing (only if consent is given)

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and analytics tools (Google Analytics, pixels) to:

  • Understand user behavior
  • Improve performance
  • Measure marketing effectiveness

You may disable cookies via your browser settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal data.

We may share information with:

  • Trusted service providers
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent for marketing

Requests can be made via our website.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

8. International Users

By using our services, you consent to your data being processed in India or other jurisdictions where we operate.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page.
